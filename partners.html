W Design Studios is based off of 5 students from the University of Washington Tacoma trying to make a contribution to the community using technology. We partner up with individuals and groups that understand our goals and make it a reality. We choose to work with others that can offer precise information that we need to answers the questions that we have and also the community as well. We hope to branch out and partner up with others in the future.
