Who We Are

We are W Design Studios. We strive to help those in need of medical supplies attain them in a time that they're needed the most. How is this done? Through the power of distributed manufacturing and 3d-printing. We will collaborate with the 3D printing community to deliver supplies to those in need. We offer the necessary files to print said supplies and directions to properly sanitize to FDA regulations.


Mission Statement

Working with University of Washington Tacoma faculty, we will create a multimedia website to provide information about and promote 3D printing of essential medical equipment during a national emergency. Covid-19 has effectively paused our communities. Looking to help, we are constantly looking for new supplies that may be of any help whatsoever and are seeking the collaboration of anyone with a 3D printer with similar goals.


Quality Assurance

All offerings have been tested and assured to be safe under the regulations specified in the files. Furthermore, we've consulted and cleared the specifications by which each product should be printed with UW Tacoma School of Engineering and Technology Faculty. We hope to collaborate with organizations with "clean rooms," so we can direct our users to easier disinfection up to standard. These supplies will make it to healthcare professionals and everyone from the young to the old, so rest assured, we're doing everything we can on our end to provide as much safety advice and specifications as possible.


Our Offerings


Face Shield.

Face shields are usually used by those in healthcare to protect the entire face from a variety of methods of spreading disease. Unlike a mask, it can block liquids, solids and residue and is more effective at stopping anything from making unintended contact with the facial region.
