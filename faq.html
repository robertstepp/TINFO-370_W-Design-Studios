3D printing is an additive manufacturing process. Different materials are heated up to around their melting temperature and deposited on a plate (usually glass or metal). This process is normally used for the rapid prototyping of items to verify that the part or print will work as intended or fits the dimensions needed.

Polylactic Acid (PLA)

  • Easy to print
  • Faster to print
  • Low cost

Polyethylene Terephthalate with Glycol (PETG)

  • Resists high temperature
  • Water proof

Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene (ABS)

  • Low cost
  • Resists high temperature
  • Wear resistant
